# Sourcing Partners Website Update

## Goal

Make Meridian's equipment sourcing claim more concrete by naming companies Meridian works with when helping international buyers find and export machinery from the United States.

## Public Framing

Use precise wording: "Companies We Work With" and "Dealer & Auction Network." Avoid stronger claims such as "exclusive partner," "authorized partner," or "official partner" unless those companies approve public co-marketing language.

## Placement

- Homepage: add a compact partner row inside the existing partner strip near the top of the page.
- Equipment Sourcing service page: add a dedicated section explaining how Meridian coordinates listings, pickup, export packing, ocean freight, and documents.

## Companies

- Randall Bros
- Sloan Implement
- Mowrey Auction
- Pfeifer's Machinery Sales

## Design Notes

Keep the section text-first. Use small cards and name chips rather than logos for the first release. This provides credibility without implying formal logo-use permission or exclusive commercial rights.
